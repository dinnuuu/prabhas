Uppalapati Venkata Suryanarayana Prabhas Raju, known mononymously as Prabhas, is an Indian actor who
works predominantly in Telugu cinema. One of the highest-paid actors in Indian cinema, Prabhas has featured in
Forbes India's Celebrity 100 list three times since 2015 based on his income and popularity.
